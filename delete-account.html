Delete your account

Last updated: 22 July 2026

This page explains how to delete your ParamPure account and personal data, and exactly what happens when you do. It applies to the ParamPure app published by ParamPure Supplies Pvt Ltd. You do not need an account to read this page.

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app (fastest)

  1. Open the ParamPure app and sign in.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete account & data.
  4. Read the confirmation screen, then confirm.
  5. For your security you may be asked to verify your mobile number by OTP again before the deletion runs.

Deletion is immediate and permanent. You will be signed out as soon as it completes.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can no longer sign in — for example you have lost access to your mobile number — email niknikhilav@gmail.com from the address on your account, with the subject "Delete my account", and include the mobile number you registered with.

We will verify that the request genuinely comes from you before deleting anything, and we will complete the deletion within 30 days. We will confirm by email when it is done.

What is deleted

  • Your profile — name, mobile number, shop name, business details and GSTIN.
  • All saved delivery addresses.
  • Your notifications and notification tokens, so we can no longer message you.
  • Your wallet ledger and any other records stored under your account.
  • Your login credentials. Your ParamPure sign-in ceases to exist.
  • Your name, shop name, address and phone number are removed from every past order.

What is retained, and why

We cannot delete the invoice and transaction records behind your past orders. Indian tax and commercial law requires sellers to keep books of account and GST invoices — typically for eight years. These records are retained in anonymised form: the order and its tax figures remain, but the buyer is shown only as "Deleted User", with no name, address or phone number attached.

Those anonymised records cannot be traced back to you, and cannot be used to re-create your account.

How long it takes

  • In-app deletion — immediate.
  • Email request — within 30 days of us verifying your identity.
  • Encrypted backups — deleted data may persist in our encrypted backups until those backups rotate out of retention. It is not accessible during that window and is never restored to the live service.

This cannot be undone

There is no recovery period and no way to restore a deleted account. If you have unpaid credit dues or an order in progress, settle or cancel it first — deleting your account does not cancel an order already dispatched, and does not clear money you owe a seller.
