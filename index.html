What ParamPure does

A dedicated ordering channel for foodservice beverages

Small food and beverage outlets in India today order tea, coffee, dairy, and packaging over phone calls, WhatsApp messages, and walk-in visits to local distributors — with no price transparency, no order history, and no proper tax invoice. ParamPure replaces that with a structured app.

For a tea stall, cafe, or restaurant owner

Browse a live catalog from suppliers who deliver to your pincode, see bulk-quantity pricing tiers up front, place an order in a few taps, track it through packed → out for delivery → delivered, and download a proper GST tax invoice the moment it's fulfilled — no more chasing a supplier for a bill at month-end.

For a wholesale supplier

List your catalog once, set bulk-pricing tiers and a service area by pincode, manage incoming orders from a dashboard with daily revenue and a 7-day trend, and export GSTR-1 JSON or Tally-ready filings for your accountant at the end of the month — without manually re-typing every invoice.

Features

Everything a B2B beverage order needs

Built specifically around how tea stalls, cafes, and small restaurants actually restock.

📦

Bulk-tier pricing

Every product can carry quantity-based price breaks — the app applies the best tier automatically as the buyer's cart quantity grows.

🧾

GST-compliant invoicing

Tax invoices with HSN codes, CGST/SGST/IGST split, and sequential invoice numbers are generated automatically per order, per seller.

📊

GSTR-1 & Tally export

Sellers download a ready-to-upload GSTR-1 JSON or a Tally-importable sales XML for any month, quarter, or financial year.

🚚

Live order tracking

Every order moves through a clear status timeline from confirmation to delivery, with pincode-based service-area checks at checkout.

💳

Wallet & flexible payment

Pay online or cash-on-delivery, track wallet credit from refunds and promotions, and reorder a saved "usual" basket in one tap.

🔐

OTP-secured accounts

Sign in with a mobile number and one-time password — no passwords to manage, with one account able to hold both buyer and seller roles.
