Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 April 2026

1. What we collect

Account basics (name, mobile, business details, GST where shared), delivery addresses, order history, device/FCM tokens for notifications, and app interaction events needed to operate the service. We do not sell your personal data.

2. How we use it

To authenticate you, fulfill orders, send transactional alerts, prevent fraud, improve the catalog, and — only if you opt in — send promotional messages. You can toggle notification channels in Profile → Notifications at any time.

3. Sharing

We share necessary order details with sellers and delivery partners to complete your order. Payment data is handled by regulated gateways (e.g. Razorpay) — we do not store full card numbers. We share with government authorities only when legally required.

4. Retention

We retain account and transaction data while your account is active and for the period required by tax/commercial law (typically 7 years in India). You can request deletion of optional data by emailing support@parampure.app.

5. Your rights

You can access, correct, or export your data, and withdraw consent for promotional messages. Write to privacy@parampure.app for any request; we respond within 30 days.

6. Security

We use transport encryption (TLS), role-based access, and OTP-based login. No system is bulletproof — notify us at security@parampure.app if you suspect a breach.

7. Children

ParamPure is not directed at children under 18 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

8. Changes

We will notify you in-app when this policy materially changes. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.

Questions? Email support@parampure.app
